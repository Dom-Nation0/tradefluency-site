Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the TradeFluency team (“we,” “us”) handles information in connection with the TradeFluency mobile app (“the App”). By using the App you agree to this policy.

The short version: TradeFluency is built to be private by design. There are no accounts, and your learning progress never leaves your phone. The only information ever transmitted is a feedback message — and only if you choose to send one.

Information we collect

The App works without an account. Your learning progress — streaks, XP, completed lessons, badges, practice-trade balance, and settings — is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to us.

We do not collect your name, email address, contacts, photos, or location, and the App contains no advertising and no analytics or tracking tools.

Feedback you send us

The App includes an optional “Send Feedback” form. If you use it, the message you type — and contact details only if you choose to include them — is transmitted to us through Web3Forms, a form-delivery service, which emails it to our inbox. We use feedback solely to respond to you and improve the App, and we do not sell or share it. Web3Forms’ handling of submissions is described in its own privacy policy at web3forms.com.

Notifications

The App can show a daily streak reminder. These notifications are scheduled and shown entirely on your device; no information is sent anywhere, and you can turn them off any time in the App’s settings.

Practice trading simulator

The App’s practice-trading feature uses simulated prices generated on your device. It involves no real money, no market data connection, and no transmission of any information.

Purchases

The App is currently free. If paid subscriptions are offered in the future, they will be processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We would never receive or store your payment-card details; the stores may share limited purchase information with us solely to provide and validate a subscription. Their handling of your data is governed by Apple’s and Google’s own privacy policies.

Children

The App is educational content about financial markets and is intended for users aged 18 and over. It is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your choices

Because your progress is stored locally, you can erase it at any time using “Reset all progress” in the Profile screen, or by deleting the App. Notifications can be disabled in the App’s settings. Any future subscription could be managed or cancelled in your App Store or Google Play account settings.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Contact us at contact@tradefluency.app.

Support

TradeFluency is a gamified app for learning how the stock market works — bite-sized lessons, quizzes, flashcards, and a risk-free practice trading simulator.

The fastest way to reach us is the Send Feedback button inside the app (Profile → Send feedback). Found a bug, have an idea, or something confused you? We read every message.

You can also email us directly at contact@tradefluency.app — we aim to reply within a few days.

Common questions

Is my progress backed up? Your progress is stored on your device for privacy. Deleting the app deletes your progress, so keep that in mind before uninstalling.

Why didn’t I get my streak reminder? Check that notifications are enabled both in the app (Profile → Settings → Streak reminders) and in your phone’s notification settings for TradeFluency.

Is this financial advice? No. TradeFluency is for education only — nothing in the app is a recommendation to buy or sell any security. Trading involves substantial risk of loss.

Disclaimer: TradeFluency is an educational app. It does not provide financial, investment, tax, or legal advice. Charts in the app are illustrative drawings for teaching pattern recognition, not real market data.
